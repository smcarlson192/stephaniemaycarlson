OPTO Business Development & Tradeshow Booth Creation

Throughout each year, OPTO tries to send out at least one direct mailing a quarter as well as participates in different tradeshows.

Tools Used: Illustrator, InDesign

Direct Mailing Campaigns

It is the graphic designer's role to come up with innovative and engaging mailings that will get the clients attention time and time again. Whether the piece is generic (such as the capabilities hook brochure) or geared towards a specific demographic (the sports cross brochure), the brochure has to catch the audience's attention and make them want to continue to read and learn more about our product.

GlobalShop 2019

Every year, OPTO participates in a number of retail design, planning and merchandising Expos. This year I collaborated with a number of Engineers and Interior Designers in creating the exterior fabric graphics, laser cut metal wall designs, and interior graphics for our 40'x20' booth. Inspired by the 1920's, the booth has a speakeasy, Art Deco and Art Nouveau feel, mixing our brand with the angled lines of our CAD files. The first half interior is meant to show how our fixtures can be utilized in a more elegant, feminine popup fashion at an airport, whereas the back shows how the fixtures can be used in a bar type environment. The booth as a whole ended up winning the 2019 Outstanding Booth Award.
