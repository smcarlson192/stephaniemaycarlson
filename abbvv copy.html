AbbVie Launch Communication Animated Videos

A newer aspect of my role at AbbVie is to create launch communication videos for internal sites to add awareness of new tools and resources that AbbVie employees can utilize.

Tools Used: Illustrator, After Effects

As of right now, I have created 9 short videos (each under 2 minutes) for 8 different program areas. To start each project off, I was provided with a script and a high-level storyboard from the team, which we then discussed and worked out the various details and themes that the clients wanted the video to possibly follow. For instance, the LINK team wanted a “Lock & Key” approach, and the nRoll team wanted a “Rock'n'Roll” theme. From there, I worked on creating a more detailed storyboard that included graphics I would use. Most videos had about 3 drafts, which were created using Adobe Illustrator and Adobe After Effects, and after each iteration the team would provide feedback and let me know if a change of direction was needed. Once the third draft was complete, if there is a voice over, it was then ordered with the official script and placed in the video file after it was received. Once everything was synced and approved, the video would be added to a site where a link can be shared to the desired audience. For some of the videos, an email campaign, a sharepoint site brand refresh, or a logo design also accompanied these different projects.
