Lake Arlington Facility Rebranding

Lake Arlington is one of the many facilities that people like to enjoy in Arlington Heights.

Tools Used: Illustrator, InDesign

For this project, I acted as project manager. I created the logo, style guide, directional and way-finding materials, as well as creating a main ground sign and interpretive signagethat goes around the facility to teach residents all about the lake. I, also, contacted vendors for estimates and worked with the winners to ensure all signage was installed correctly and on time. For the reopening event, I was in charge of creating educational material on the available paths Arlington Heights has to offer and create all advertisements for the event.
