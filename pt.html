PicTale App & Website FE Development

Unveil Your Story, One Snapshot at a Time.

PicTale is a digital scrapbook, seamlessly organizing life's moments - from cherished photos to heartfelt notes - into a personalized narrative. Preserving memories for generations to come.

My Role:UX/UI Designer & Front End Developer

Members:4

Time:2 weeks

Tools Used:Trello, Figma, Adobe Illustrator, VS Code, GitHub

Problem

People often struggle to organize and preserve their life memories, including photos, videos, and significant moments. As time passes, these memories may become scattered and at risk of being lost as the chaos of life takes over.

Solution

Our solution is to create an app that allows users the ability to upload videos, pictures, thoughts, notes and everything in between to create almost a “digital scrapbook” of events that happen.

Design Process

Qualitative Research

We aimed to collect qualitative data in order to design an app that caters to users who want to organize their digital memories in a fun and easy way. We prepared 20 questions to ask and conducted semi-structured interviews with 5 people between the ages of 25 and 35. We wanted to better understand how users organize their photo & video memories, the issues users may face when organizing their memories, and how users would want to integrate memory sharing with privacy.

Final Thoughts

After analyzing the feedback I received from my user tests, I was able to go back and make updates to all the high priority issues for the users and TravelGlass making the app a better experience for all involved.

Users were very excited about the prospect of this app and could see using it every time they plan a trip.

TravelGlass is a collaborative itinerary planning tool that allows users to have an organized trip from start to finish with their travel partners fully involved. TravelGlass also provides peace of mind for loved ones back home who can see the traveler's location in real-time and are notified if any delays or emergencies come up. Travelers using TravelGlass can feel at ease knowing that if an emergency situation comes up back home, someone will know who to contact.
