Rebrand + Package Redesign

This is a rebranding of a product that is used on a daily basis. The task was to conduct research and figure out ways to make this product packaging more appealing to users as well as find a way to improve its branding.

Tools Used: Illustrator, InDesign

I, first, created a consumption log of how many times I used this product and then researched it to get important facts about the object. After I created an infographic depicting some of this information, along with a graphic style manual, and advertisement that follows a similar design style, and a mock-up or rendering of the new packaging design.
