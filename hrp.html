Hidden Red Panda

Hidden Red Panda is a fully playable board game designed based on an animal that is becoming extinct. Every component was designed, including the packaging, playing board and cards, game pieces, instruction manual and the pregame information packet.

Tools Used: Illustrator, 3D Printer, Laser Cutter, InDesign

The Preface of the game is:

"You are a Red Panda living in the high temperate forests of the Eastern Himalayas. Red Pandas are becoming extinct and it is your job to try to find your friends and save the rest of your species. You will have some obstacles in your way, so try to watch out for predators, deforestation and animal traps! Your goal is to split up from your friends, find your mate and make it back home before they do."

This game is meant to teach people about the habits, life, and environment of the Red Panda.
